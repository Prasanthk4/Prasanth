About Me

Hello! I am Sai Rama Prasanth, a passionate Data Analyst and Front-end Developer based in Hyderabad. With a background in Business Analytics and a keen interest in technology, I thrive on transforming data into actionable insights that can drive informed decisions. My academic journey, complemented by hands-on projects, has equipped me with a solid foundation in data analysis, machine learning, and statistics. I am always eager to learn and explore new trends in the field of analytics and development.

As a Front-end Developer, I enjoy creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. I have a strong understanding of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which I use to develop responsive web applications. My goal is to combine my analytical skills with my front-end development expertise to deliver solutions that not only look great but also provide a seamless user experience. I am excited about the future of technology and am always on the lookout for new challenges and opportunities to grow in this dynamic field.
